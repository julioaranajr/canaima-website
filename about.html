About me

Biography

Hello! My name is Julio Arana. I was born in the beautiful country of Venezuela. Over the years, I have had the incredible opportunity to live in various countries including Mexico, USA, UK, Spain, and Germany. These experiences have enriched my life and broadened my perspectives. Citizen of the world, Warrior of the Light and Promoter of the Universal Culture.

Professional Background

I am a dedicated Cloud Systems Developer with a passion for technology and innovation. I am proud to hold three AWS certifications, which attest to my expertise and commitment to staying at the forefront of cloud computing. Currently, I am single and focusing on my career and personal growth. I am also a DCI student learning Python to further enhance my skills and keep up with the latest advancements in technology.

Personal Interests

One of my great passions is piloting drones, and I love to go outdoors to film beautiful landscapes.

Traveling is one of my greatest passions. I love exploring new places, immersing myself in different cultures, and meeting new people. Sports are also a big part of my life, keeping me active and energized. Sports play a significant role in my life. I am an avid Real Madrid fan, eagerly hoping for another Champions League victory.

As a coffee lover, I enjoy discovering unique coffee shops and savoring various coffee blends from around the world. Additionally, I have a deep appreciation for international cuisine and love trying new dishes.

Family

I am a proud father of three wonderful kids who bring immense joy and fulfillment to my life. Watching them grow and supporting their dreams is my greatest achievement.
